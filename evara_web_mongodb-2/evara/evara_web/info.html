About Us

Evara is an online fashion store founded in 2018 with a simple goal: to bring trend-led, quality clothing and accessories to everyone at fair prices. From everyday basics to statement pieces, every product is hand-picked by our team.

Today we serve over 50,000 happy customers across the country, shipping from our warehouse in Beb-Wahren with a focus on speed, sustainability, and customer care.

Delivery Information

  • Standard delivery: 3–5 business days — FREE on orders over ₹1,000 (₹49 otherwise).
  • Express delivery: 1–2 business days — ₹99 flat.
  • Orders are processed within 24 hours and shipped Monday–Saturday.
  • You'll receive a tracking link by email once your order is dispatched.
  • We currently deliver across India. International shipping is coming soon.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. The information you share with us — your name, email, and shipping address — is used solely to process your orders and improve your shopping experience.

  • We never sell or rent your personal data to third parties.
  • Passwords are stored securely using one-way encryption.
  • You can request deletion of your account and data at any time.
  • Cookies are used only to keep your cart and session working.

Terms & Conditions

By using the Evara website you agree to the following terms:

  • All prices are listed in Indian Rupees (₹) and include applicable taxes.
  • Products may be returned within 7 days of delivery if unused and with tags intact.
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.
  • Evara reserves the right to update prices and offers without prior notice.
  • This is a demo store — payments are simulated and no real charges are made.
